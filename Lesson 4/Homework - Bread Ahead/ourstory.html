Our Story

From humble beginnings as a part-time worker at her local High Street Greggs, Juliana Rye fell in love with all things loaf, igniting in her the desire to create the ultimate bread roll.

After a gap year travelling around Europe and working in various kitchens, secretly observing the methods of artisinal bakers without having to actually pay for a course, Juliana was ready to gentrify the entire bread industry, returning back to the West Midlands with the family recipe of her now famous pumpernickle loaf (never mind the recipe is not actually from her family, but the Italian grandma whose recipe card she nicked when she had her back turned really helped inspire Juliana).

Having started out from a food truck, generously seed funded by her parents and their various associates at the club, Juliana now owns a cute cafe in the indie corner, where she still works on the day to day creations of various sourdough starters and lemon poppy scones.
